About Us

Excellent Co. is a family-owned painting and remodeling business proudly serving the DFW area of Texas for over 25 years. From the very beginning, our mission has been to deliver exceptional craftsmanship and customer service, whether working on a small home project or a large commercial renovation. Our dedicated team takes pride in treating every project as if it were for our own family, ensuring quality, reliability, and a modern touch in everything we do.
